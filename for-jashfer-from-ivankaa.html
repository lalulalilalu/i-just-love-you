A Letter for You, My Sayang

Dear Jashfer, Sometimes I try to explain why I love you so much, but the truth is, I never can. There are a hundred reasons, maybe more, but none of them ever feel enough. Still, I’ll try. I love the way you make me feel calm, even when I’m being dramatic. You never make me feel like I’m too much, even when I know I can be. You just stay — steady, patient, soft. You let me be me. And that’s one of the reasons I’ll always be grateful for you. I love how your presence feels like peace. The world could be loud and messy, but when it’s just you and me, everything slows down. You make ordinary days feel special. You make silence comfortable. You make life lighter. You listen. You care in quiet ways. You notice small things I don’t even realize about myself. You remember what I like, how I take my coffee, when I need space, or when I just need a hug. Those small things — they mean everything to me. I love how you love me — without pressure, without games, without conditions. You love me on my soft days and my loud days, when I’m smiling and when I’m overthinking. You love all my sides, even the ones I sometimes wish I could hide. And Sayang, I love the way you make me laugh. How you never let me stay upset for long. How you tease me just enough to bring me back to myself. You make life feel lighter, brighter, and so much more beautiful. With you, everything feels right. You’re my calm after my chaos, my safety after a storm. You’re the warmth I look for, the voice that grounds me, the heart that feels like home. Thank you for always being patient with me. For never making me feel like I have to earn your love. For staying — not just in words, but in the quiet, constant ways that truly matter. I don’t love you because you’re perfect. I love you because you’re real. Because you choose to love me every single day, even when I’m not the easiest to love. So here’s my truth, simple and sure: I love you, Jashfer. I love you for a hundred reasons and none at all. I love you because you’re you — and that’s all I ever need. Always, your Ivankaa
